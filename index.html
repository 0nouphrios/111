Important Social and Global Importance Days

This website lists some of the most important social and global importance days.

  • Pride Month
  • International Women's Day
  • World Health Day
  • International Children's Day
  • Earth Day
  • World Mental Health Day
  • International Day of Persons with Disabilities

Pride Month

Pride Month is celebrated in June each year to commemorate the 1969 Stonewall Riots, a pivotal moment in the gay rights movement.

Learn more about Pride Month

International Women's Day

International Women's Day is celebrated on March 8th each year to celebrate the achievements of women and to raise awareness of the challenges they still face.

Learn more about International Women's Day

World Health Day

World Health Day is celebrated on April 7th each year to raise awareness of global health issues and to promote action to improve health outcomes for all.

Learn more about World Health Day

International Children's Day

International Children's Day is celebrated on November 20th each year to promote the well-being of children around the world.

Learn more about International Children's Day

Earth Day

Earth Day is celebrated on April 22nd each year to raise awareness of environmental issues and to promote action to protect the planet.

Learn more about Earth Day

World Mental Health Day

World Mental Health Day is celebrated on October 10th each year to raise awareness of mental health issues and to break down the stigma associated with them.

Learn more about World Mental Health Day

International Day of Persons with Disabilities

International Day of Persons with Disabilities is celebrated on December 3rd each year to promote the rights and inclusion of people with disabilities.

Learn more about International Day of Persons with Disabilities
